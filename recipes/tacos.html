Tacos

This is a amazing recipe of Mexican tacos with a traditional ingredients.

This is a perfect food to eat at breakfast lunch or dinner as a Mexican style.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg tortilla taco
  • 400g pork meat
  • 200g pineapple
  • 2 onions
  • Coriander
  • Pastor sauce

Steps

  1. Slice the pork meat in small pieces and fried it
  2. cook the tortillas adding a little bit of oil
  3. chop the onions
  4. Chop the Coriander
  5. Chop the pineapple
